Announcement: Speakers and Sponsors

  • by miyagawa

Shibuya Perl Mongers are happy to announce the chosen speakers and our sponsors for YAPC::Asia 2007.

YAPC::Asia 2007 Tokyo

Date
April 4-5th, 2007 (Wed-Thu) 9:30-18:00
Venue
Tsuda Hall, Sendagaya, Tokyo
Fee
4000 JPY (2 Days including T-shirt)
Organizer
YAPC::Asia 2007 Committee / Shibuya Perl Mongers
Registration
Register Now
