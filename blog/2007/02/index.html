February 02, 2007

yapcasia2007 -- the official tag for YAPC::Asia 2007

Tagging your posts, photos and videos is a clever way to make it really easy for people to find blog posts or media assets online. Here we announce the official tag for YAPC::Asia 2007, which is yapcasia2007.

Tag yapcasia2007 for your posts (on Technorati, del.icio.us or Hatena Bookmark), photos (on Flickr) and videos (on YouTube) and everyone can find your stuff easily and quickly. Thanks!

February 01, 2007

CFP closed -- Thanks for the proposals!

Now it's February 1st based on the UTC time and we closed the Call For Papers submissions. Thanks for the massive submissions!

We have 1.5 times more proposals than actually we have slots for, hence all we can say is your talks will be screened from now on and we'll let you know the result not later than Febrary 14th via Email.

We will start CFP for the Lightning Talks (5 minutes, 11 talks) in the late March, so if you missed the chance to submit a full proposal but have something you can talk in 5 minutes, wait for another chance.

Thanks!
